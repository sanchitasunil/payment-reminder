## About The Clinic

The Clinic is a general practice clinic in Maplewood. We were founded in 2018 with a mission to provide accessible, honest primary healthcare for local families. We are not a hospital and we do not offer emergency services. We care for around three hundred regular patients and focus on continuity of care, clear communication, and practical advice you can follow at home.

## Location and how to find us

Our address is 14 Birch Lane, Suite 2, Maplewood. We are on the second floor of the Birchwood Medical Building. Free parking is available in the building lot. Street parking is also available on Birch Lane when the lot is full. Search for The Clinic Maplewood on Google Maps to get directions to the building entrance.

## Clinic hours

For clinic timings, opening hours, and when we are open: we are open Monday to Saturday from nine in the morning to one in the afternoon, and from five in the evening to eight at night. We are closed on Sundays and on public holidays. The last appointment is accepted thirty minutes before closing time. Walk-ins are welcome when slots are available, but waiting time may be longer than for patients who have booked in advance.

## Booking and cancellation policy

Appointments are thirty minutes long. Please arrive five minutes early. Cancel at least two hours before your appointment time — we cannot accommodate late cancellations. Rescheduling is free once. A second reschedule within the same week incurs a small admin charge. Walk-ins are welcome subject to availability on the day.

## Consultation fees

A general consultation costs fifty dollars. A follow-up visit for the same condition within two weeks costs thirty-five dollars. A specialist consultation costs eighty dollars. Home visits are not currently available.

## Payments we accept

We accept cash and all major debit and credit cards. For health insurance, we work with most major providers — please confirm your coverage before your visit. For insurers we do not partner with directly, patients pay at the clinic and claim reimbursement from their provider.

## Lab and diagnostic services

We offer in-clinic lab tests including complete blood count, blood sugar fasting and random, HbA1c, lipid profile, thyroid panel covering T3 T4 and TSH, liver function tests, kidney function tests, and urine analysis. Most results are available the same day by six in the evening. HbA1c results are ready the following morning. We do not have X-ray, ECG, or ultrasound on-site — we will refer you to a nearby diagnostic centre if needed.

## Pharmacy

We have a small in-clinic pharmacy stocking common medicines, first-aid supplies, and over-the-counter medications. A full-service pharmacy is a short walk from the building.

## Parking

Free parking is available in the building lot. Street parking is also available on Birch Lane when the lot is full.

## Emergencies and urgent care

The Clinic is not an emergency facility. For any medical emergency, call your local emergency number immediately or go to the nearest emergency room. For urgent but non-emergency concerns, call us first on our clinic number and we will advise whether you should come in or go to hospital.
